Refund Policy for autophoto.store

Last updated: April 14, 2026

This Refund Policy applies to all digital Products sold on autophoto.store (including AI photo/video tools, Chrome extensions, and digital plugins) (the "Products"). By purchasing our Products, you agree to the terms of this policy.（本退款政策适用于autophoto.store上销售的所有数字产品（包括AI摄影/视频工具、Chrome插件和数字插件）（以下简称“产品”）。购买我们的产品，即表示你同意本政策条款。）

1. Nature of Digital Products（数字产品性质）

All Products on autophoto.store are digital, non-physical goods with instant delivery upon payment confirmation (e.g., immediate access to download links, activation codes). Due to the intangible nature of digital products, which are non-returnable and non-refundable once delivered (and subject to immediate duplication), we do not offer unconditional or "no reason" refunds.（autophoto.store上的所有产品均为“数字、非实物商品”，付款确认后即时交付（如立即获取下载链接、激活码）。由于数字产品的无形性，交付后不可退回、不可退款（且可立即复制），因此我们不提供无条件或“无理由”退款。）

2. Eligibility for Refunds（退款资格）

Refunds will only be considered and approved in the following limited circumstances:（仅在以下“有限情况下”，我们会考虑并批准退款：）

  • The Product is technically defective and cannot be activated or used as described on the Website, and our customer service team is unable to resolve the issue within 24 business hours.（产品存在技术缺陷，无法按网站描述激活或使用，且我们的客户服务团队无法在24个工作日内解决该问题。）
  • Accidental duplicate payment for the same Product (you must provide proof of duplicate transaction from your payment provider).（同一产品意外重复付款（你需提供支付服务商出具的重复交易证明）。）
  • The wrong Product was delivered due to an error on our part (you must notify us within 1 hour of payment).（因我们的失误导致交付错误产品（你需在付款后1小时内通知我们）。）

3. Refund Request Process（退款申请流程）

To request a refund, you must:（申请退款，你需：）

  1. Send an email to tourinn@gmail.com with the subject line "Refund Request - [Your Order Number]".（发送邮件至tourinn@gmail.com，主题格式为“Refund Request - [你的订单号]”。）
  2. Include the following information: your full name, email address used for payment, order number, and clear proof of the issue (e.g., screenshot of technical error, transaction receipt for duplicate payment).（包含以下信息：你的全名、付款所用邮箱、订单号，以及问题的清晰证明（如技术错误截图、重复交易收据）。）
  3. Submit the request within 48 hours of payment (requests received after 48 hours will not be considered).（在付款后“48小时内”提交申请（超过48小时收到的申请将不被考虑）。）

4. Refund Processing（退款处理）

  • Approved refunds will be processed through Stripe, the original payment method used for the purchase.（批准的退款将通过购买时使用的原始支付方式（Stripe）处理。）
  • Refund processing times are determined by your payment provider (typically 3-7 business days for credit/debit cards, up to 10 business days for bank transfers).（退款处理时间由你的支付服务商决定（信用卡通常为3-7个工作日，银行转账最长为10个工作日）。）
  • No refunds will be issued for requests that do not meet the eligibility criteria above, or for issues caused by user error (e.g., incorrect email address, incompatible device/software, accidental deletion of download links).（不符合上述退款资格的申请，或因用户操作失误导致的问题（如邮箱错误、设备/软件不兼容、误删下载链接），将不予以退款。）

5. No Chargebacks Without Prior Contact（未经联系不得发起拒付）

If you dispute a charge (initiate a chargeback) with your payment provider without first contacting us to resolve the issue, your access to all our Products will be immediately revoked, and future purchases on autophoto.store will be prohibited. We will contest all unjustified chargebacks with Stripe and provide evidence of order fulfillment.（如你未先联系我们解决问题，便向支付服务商发起拒付争议，你的所有产品访问权限将立即被撤销，且禁止未来在autophoto.store购买产品。我们将通过Stripe抗辩所有不合理拒付，并提供订单履行证明。）
